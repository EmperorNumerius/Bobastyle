History of Airplanes

The history of airplanes dates back to the early 20th century. The Wright brothers are credited with inventing and building the world's first successful airplane in 1903. Since then, aviation technology has advanced significantly, leading to the development of various types of aircraft for different purposes, including commercial, military, and recreational use.

Types of Airplanes

Airplanes come in various types, each designed for specific purposes. Commercial airplanes are used for transporting passengers and cargo. Military aircraft include fighters, bombers, and transport planes. There are also private jets, gliders, and ultralight aircraft designed for recreational flying.
